Purpose

To provide a reliable, accessible tool for authorities and researchers to analyze traffic data efficiently. We aim to make roads safer by offering a solution for speed violation detection and accident prevention

About Our Platform

Our platform leverages advanced machine learning and computer vision techniques to deliver accurate vehicle speed estimation from video footage. We aim to revolutionize traffic monitoring by providing real-time insights that can be used for safety, compliance, and efficiency purposes.

Our Technology

We utilize state-of-the-art algorithms like Optical Flow and the Lucas-Kanade Tracker to analyze vehicle motion in video footage. This allows us to calculate precise speed estimates, whether in live feeds or pre-recorded video, ensuring a reliable analysis for diverse applications.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve traffic monitoring and road safety through innovative technology. By enabling faster, more accurate analysis of vehicle speeds, we aim to support traffic management, reduce accidents, and enhance road safety for communities worldwide.

Why Choose Us?

We provide an intuitive, user-friendly interface backed by powerful algorithms to make complex data accessible to everyone. Whether for government traffic management or private sector needs, our platform offers reliability, efficiency, and accuracy in speed estimation
